How to Play Checkers

  • Objective: Capture all opponent pieces or block them so they cannot move.
  • Movement: Pieces always move diagonally forward one square (unless capturing).
  • Capturing: Jump over an opponent's adjacent piece into an empty square. Captures are mandatory.
  • Multi-Jumps: If a jump lands in a position where another jump is possible by the same piece, it must continue jumping.
  • Kings: When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a King (ðŸ‘‘). Kings can move and jump both forward and backward.
